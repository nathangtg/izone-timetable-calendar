Privacy Notice for Sunway iZone to Timetable

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

What We Collect

Nothing. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: To access your current iZone page
  • downloads: To export the timetable as a .ics file
  • host permissions: Only for https://izone.sunway.edu.my
  • scripting: To read timetable data from the DOM

Data Usage Policy

We do not and will never sell or share any user data. The extension is purpose-built for converting timetables to calendar files.
